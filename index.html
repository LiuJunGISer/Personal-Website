Subtropical High Intensity during Warm Periods and Its Influence on Precipitation in Eastern China

Undergraduate Thesis Research

  • Analyzed spatiotemporal variability of precipitation anomalies using observational datasets and NCEP/ERA5 reanalysis data.
  • Quantified changes in the intensity and spatial extent of the Western Pacific Subtropical High and related circulation patterns.
  • Explored links among moisture transport, atmospheric circulation anomalies, ENSO conditions, and regional precipitation variability.

Three-Dimensional Urban Spatial Morphology and Urban Heat Island Effects

Core Team Member | Provincial Undergraduate Innovation Project

  • Performed single-tree segmentation from high-resolution remote sensing imagery to extract urban vegetation structure.
  • Developed a Python framework to derive three-dimensional landscape metrics from canopy height model data.
  • Integrated 3D morphology indicators with land surface temperature data and machine learning models including Random Forest and XGBoost.

Residential Patterns and Household Carbon Emissions

Research Assistant | University Innovation Project

  • Applied Structural Equation Modeling to analyze pathways linking urban spatial structure and household carbon emissions.
